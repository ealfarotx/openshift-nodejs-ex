Assign an overpayment

When you begin working on an overpayment, you should assign the overpayment to yourself to let other users know you are working on the overpayment.

  • Click Assign to me in the overpayment card (summary or detail view). The Assign to me link only displays in the overpayment card if the overpayment is not assigned to anyone.
  • Click the action menu in the overpayment card (summary or detail view), and then click Assign to me. If the overpayment is assigned to another user, you must use the Assign to me link in the action menu .
When an overpayment is assigned to a user, the user's name displays in the overpayment card. You can locate overpayments assigned to you using the Search By fields near the top of the Overpayments page. Select Assigned To in the first search field, and then select Self in the second search field.

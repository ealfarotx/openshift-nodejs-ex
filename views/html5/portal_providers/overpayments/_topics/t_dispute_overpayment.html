Dispute an overpayment

Overpayments: Submit a dispute for an overpayment to the payer.

If you disagree with an overpayment, you can submit a dispute for the overpayment to the payer. You can only dispute overpayments that have a status of Action Required or Inquired.

  1. Click the action menu in the overpayment card, and then click Dispute Overpayment.
  2. In the Dispute Overpayment window, select a dispute type, and then type a description or reason for the dispute. The description or reason you enter will be saved as part of the overpayment's conversation history.
  3. Click Submit.
  4. In the Success window, click Close, or click Disputed Overpayments to jump to a list of disputed overpayments.

When you dispute an overpayment, the overpayment's status is changed to Disputed.

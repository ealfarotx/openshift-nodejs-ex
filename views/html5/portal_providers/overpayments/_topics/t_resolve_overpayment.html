Overpayments: Resolve an overpayment.

When a settlement about an overpayment is reached, you can resolve the overpayment. You can select from two different payment options when you resolve an overpayment:

  • Have the payer deduct the overpayment amount from a future payment (recoupment).

  • Send a check for the overpayment amount to the payer.

You can only resolve overpayments that have a status of Action Required, Inquired, or Disputed.

  1. Click the action menu in the overpayment card, and then click Resolve Overpayment.
  2. In the Resolve Overpayment window, select a resolution option:
    • Select Agree - Offset to have the overpayment recouped from a future payment.
    • Select Agree - Refund if you will be sending the payer a check for the overpayment.
  3. In the Resolve Overpayment window, select the check box, and then click Agree.
  4. In the Success window, click Close, or click Resolved Overpayments to jump to a list of resolved overpayments.
When you resolve an overpayment, the overpayment's status is changed to Resolved.
